🎓 PUPPY TRAINING TECHNIQUES

Proven methods from 40+ years of professional training experience

Core Philosophy
  • Puppy Montessori Method: Natural learning in real environments with mentor dogs guiding puppies through socialization
  • Balance: Positive reinforcement, marker training, and appropriate guidance when needed
  • Foundation First: Solid basic obedience before advanced retrieving work
Key Training Strategies
  • Inductive Retrieve: Build strong retrieving foundations through motivation and clear expectations (not force)
  • Adventure-Based Learning: Train in diverse real-world environments (trails, water, distractions) not just sterile rooms
  • Impulse Control: Teach patience and good decision-making through structured waiting and settling exercises
  • Distraction-Proofing: Gradually increase distractions (squirrels, people, other dogs) to build reliable obedience
  • Socialization Priority: Extensive environmental exposure during critical 8-16 week period
Weeks 8-12 Foundation Focus
  • Name recognition and engagement
  • Crate training with positive associations
  • House rules and boundaries (no counter surfing, furniture rules)
  • Basic commands: Sit, Here (recall), Kennel
  • Leash walking without pulling
  • Calm greetings (no jumping)
  • Environmental exposure daily (new sounds, surfaces, people)
Retrieving Development
  • Week 12-16: Start retrieving fundamentals with high motivation (toys, bumpers)
  • Inductive Method: Let puppy's natural retrieve drive develop through play and encouragement
  • Build Desire: Make retrieving the BEST game - never forced or punished
  • Short Sessions: 5-10 minutes of high-energy fun, always end wanting more
